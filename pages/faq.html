Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about our platform and tournaments.

What is Esportive?
Esportive is a dedicated platform for esports enthusiasts to discover and participate in competitive gaming tournaments across various titles.
Is it free to use the Esportive platform?
Yes, creating an account and browsing tournaments on Esportive is completely free.
How do I register for a tournament?
First, you must be signed in to your Esportive account. Then, navigate to the Tournaments page, select the tournament you wish to join, and click the Register button. It will redirect to registration destination. Follow the instructions to complete your registration.
Esportive host tournaments?
No, Esportive doesn't host or organise any tournaments. Esportive only publish tournaments of third party or official organiser.
How are prize pools distributed?
Prize pool distribution is handled directly by the tournament organizer. Esportive does not manage prize money.
Do I need a team to join a tournament?
It depends on the tournament. Some tournaments are for solo players, while others require a pre-made team or duo. The required format will be clearly specified in the tournament's details.
