Cookie Policy

1. What are Cookies?

A cookie is a small text file that is stored on your device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide a more personalized experience for the user. They allow a website to remember your actions and preferences over a period of time, such as your login information, language, or font size, so you don't have to re-enter them every time you return to the site.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To remember your login status so you don't have to sign in every time you visit a new page.
  • Security: To detect and prevent unauthorized use of your account and protect against security vulnerabilities.
  • Performance and Analytics: To collect information about how you use our website, such as which pages you visit most often, to help us analyze and improve the performance of our Service.
  • User Preferences: To store your preferences and settings, such as language preferences or display settings, to provide a more personalized experience.

3. Your Choices Regarding Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can set or amend your web browser controls to accept or refuse cookies. Most browsers allow you to manage cookies, including deleting and blocking them. If you choose to reject cookies, you may still use our website, though your access to some functionality and areas may be restricted. Since the means by which you can refuse cookies through your web browser controls vary from browser to browser, you should visit your browser's help menu for more information.

4. Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may also use various third-parties' cookies to report usage statistics of the Service, deliver advertisements on and through the Service, and so on. These third parties include, but are not limited to, Google Analytics and Firebase Authentication, which use cookies to track and analyze website usage and provide secure user authentication.
